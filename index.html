Information

Spacious, bright apartments in the very center of Altea (in the old town). 3 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms (1 with shower), spacious living room with fireplace and access to a large solarium terrace with views of the sea, the Sierra Bernia mountains and the main cathedral of the city. The apartment is just a stone's throw from the old town, where some of the best restaurants, ice cream parlors and boutiques are located. To the beach 5 minutes walk.

In the courtyard of the house there is a playground. On Tuesdays from 8-14 fruit and vegetable market. There is a 24 hour minimarket next to the house. 3 minutes walk from a large supermarket Dia and Hiperber, a little further - Mercadona.

La Marina shopping center and Carefour supermarket 15-20 minutes by car. Terra Natura Zoo, Terra Mítica Theme Park and Aqualandia are within a 20-minute drive. To Alicante airport - 50 minutes and 1.5 hours to Valencia airport.
